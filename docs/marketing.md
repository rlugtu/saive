# Saive — marketing overview

> Living marketing copy. Update this alongside `DESIGN.md` and the feature docs whenever a
> shipped feature changes what we can honestly promise. Keep it benefit-first and true to the app.

## The pitch

**Saive is where your bookmarks finally make sense — organized into shareable lists, not lost in a
sea of browser tabs.** Save any link and Saive auto-fills the name, description, and photos from
the page, so a single paste turns into a rich, browsable card. Group those cards into lists,
color-code them with tags, and share them with friends — Saive turns "I'll send you that link
later" into a living collection you build together.

Whether you're planning a trip, tracking restaurants to try, or curating research, Saive keeps the
things worth remembering in one beautiful place — on the web, or installed as an app on your phone.

## Why people love it

### 🤝 Collaborate on lists, in real partnership
Bookmarks in Saive live inside **lists you can share** — and sharing isn't just read-only. Invite
people as **viewers** (they can browse and comment) or **collaborators** (they can add, edit, and
comment right alongside you), while you stay in control as the owner. Planning a weekend away or
building the ultimate taco ranking? Everyone contributes to the same list, drops comments on
individual bookmarks, and watches it grow together — no more scattered group chats and lost links.

### 🏷️ Tags that categorize at a glance
Every bookmark can be labeled with **your own tags**, and Saive gives each new tag a color the
moment you create it — so your lists become instantly scannable, color-coded, and yours. Filter a
list by one tag or several at once to zero in on exactly what you're after ("cheap eats," "date
night," "must-see"). It's organization that feels effortless because you never have to think about
folders again.

### 📍 Find bookmarks near you
Saved a hidden gem months ago and forgot where it was? Saive's **Near me** finder uses your current
location to surface the bookmarks around you — pick a radius, and it lists everything within range,
closest first, each with the exact distance. It even shows you the address it located you at, so you
always know your bearings. Out exploring a new neighborhood, Saive quietly reminds you: *you already
saved something great, and it's two blocks away.*

## Also worth knowing
- **Paste-to-save magic** — drop in a link and Saive pulls the title, description, and images
  automatically (and spots playable videos).
- **Two gorgeous themes** — a retro 8-bit **pixel** look and a sleek **modern** look, each in light
  and dark.
- **Installable PWA** — add Saive to your home screen and use it like a native app.
